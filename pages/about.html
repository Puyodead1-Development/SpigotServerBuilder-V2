About SpigotServerBuilder

This is the second attempt at SpigotServerBuilder, the old one, made in java, is very sloppy, stiched together, and not optimized. Version 2 is much cleaner and easier to use, it is way more efficient and has way more potential. This version is made using Electron, a JavaScript and Chrome based framework.
